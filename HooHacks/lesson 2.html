How do I come up with a good idea?


This is another question that we get quite often so let's break it down in to 4 simple steps:

Brainstorming Session:

Once you have identified a common topic of interest within your team, your going to want to have a brainstorming session. In this session, you will write down any idea that comes to mind and encourage your group members to do the same. (Remember there are NO bad ideas, any idea could be good at this stage and you don't want to rule out anything)

Eliminate:

Once you feel that you have every possible idea written down, it's time to start eliminating. This means identifying pros and cons and analyzing which ideas have the most potential to make a difference in addition to which ideas you think are creative. In this stage you are trying to eliminate anything that is not creative, not solving a problem, or not achievable.

Narrow Down:

Now that you have eliminated any poor ideas, it's time to choose. This stage is going to require some heavier research in terms of pros and cons as well as potential. It is crucial to ensure that every member of the team is a part of this stage as this will allow you to avoid any conflicts regarding the idea after this stage.

Ask a Mentor:

The final step in your idea stage is getting another opinion. In hackathons you will almost always have access to mentors so try to use them to your advantage by asking them for an opinion regarding your idea as they will have different experiences that allow them to see it from another angle.
