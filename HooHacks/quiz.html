Complete the Simulated Hackathon

Help Tim! Now that you have gone through some lessons and know how to work through a hackathon, it's time to test your skills out in a game. Tim is a high school student participating through his first hackathon and it is your job to help him work through it and make the right decisions. You will have to press on the button that corresponds to the answer you think is correct. If it turns green you are correct but if it turns red you are wrong. Good Luck!
